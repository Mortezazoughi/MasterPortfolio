Mission Agpe

Touch Someone's Life. Volunteer With Us!

Mission Agape is a 501c3 Christian Charity. Our members come from many different faith, cultural, and economic backgrounds. Though we are many backgrounds, we are one family. Agape means "the highest form of love, charity". This is our name; this is our mission. To reach out to others in need. It is our hope and prayer that we'll be able to make a positive impact in those around us, both in our local community and in our global community.
